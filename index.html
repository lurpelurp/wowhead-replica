The War Within: Beginning of the Worldsoul Saga

The War Within is the tenth expansion for World of Warcraft and marks the beginning of the ambitious Worldsoul Saga. Released on August 26, 2024, this expansion takes players on a journey through never-before-seen subterranean worlds filled with hidden wonders and lurking perils, down to the dark depths of the nerubian empire.

Journey beneath the surface of Azeroth to explore the enigmatic realm of Khaz Algar, where the malicious Xal'atath, Harbinger of the Void, is gathering arachnid forces to bring Azeroth to its knees. This expansion introduces groundbreaking features while celebrating 20 years of World of Warcraft with the return of beloved characters like Thrall, Jaina Proudmoore, and Anduin Wrynn.

Key Expansion Features

  • New Level Cap: 80
  • New Allied Race: Earthen - Titan-forged beings made of living stone
  • Hero Talents: New talent trees for each class specialization
  • Warbands: Account-wide progression system
  • Delves: New endgame pillar for 1-4 players
  • Dynamic Flying: Enhanced aerial mobility system
  • 8 New Dungeons: 4 leveling, 4 max-level
  • Nerub-ar Palace Raid: 8-boss raid with Story Mode

The Story: Defend Azeroth from the Shadows Below

Deep within the kingdom of Azj-Kahet lies Nerub-ar Palace, Queen Ansurek's seat of power. From this shadowed throne, Queen Ansurek has tightened her grip on her Sureki followers, leading them along a dark path toward a new future. As her paranoia grows, members of the conspiratorial Severed Threads launch their final gambit against Ansurek's rule.

The expansion's storyline, crafted by Chris Metzen and Terran Gregory, focuses on themes of family and features the Bronzebeard clan prominently, while following Alleria Windrunner's journey as she confronts the growing Void threat.

Explore Khaz Algar: The World Beneath Azeroth

Beneath the surface of Azeroth lies an expansive subterranean world, fraught with danger and waiting to be explored. Khaz Algar offers four distinct zones, each with unique environments, inhabitants, and challenges.

Isle of Dorn (Levels 70-73)

The starting zone for the expansion, featuring sprawling mountain ranges and lush landscapes. This is the homeland of the Earthen race, with their capital city Dornogal serving as the new unified hub for both Horde and Alliance players. The zone showcases the natural beauty of the underground world with crystalline formations and ancient titan architecture.

The Ringing Deeps (Levels 73-75)

A massive cavern system that serves as home to the enigmatic Machine Speakers - Earthen who tirelessly maintain ancient Titan machines, ensuring their continued operation and guarding their secrets. This zone features towering mechanical constructs and echoing chambers filled with the sounds of ancient machinery.

Hallowfall (Levels 75-78)

A region scarred by craters and bordered by towering rock formations, illuminated by a colossal crystal at its center. This bright underground zone is home to the Arathi - a resilient people dedicated to protecting their homeland from the unrelenting Nerubian forces. The zone represents hope and light in the darkness below.

Azj-Kahet (Levels 78-80)

The pinnacle of Nerubian civilization and the center of Xal'atath's dark schemes. In this shadowy stronghold, darkness reigns supreme, with arachnid horrors poised to unleash chaos on Azeroth. This zone features the imposing Nerub-ar Palace and represents the ultimate challenge for players.

New Allied Race: The Earthen

The War Within introduces the Earthen as a new allied race available to both Alliance and Horde players. These titan-forged beings are crafted from the very essence of Azeroth, imbued with extraordinary strength, resilience, and an unyielding resolve to protect the world from looming threats.

Earthen Characteristics

  • Origins: Created by the Titans during the ancient era of world-shaping
  • Connection: Deep bond with Azeroth's worldsoul
  • Purpose: Guardians and protectors of Azeroth
  • Unlock Requirements: Complete their questline and the overall level-up campaign
  • Available Classes: Multiple class options for diverse playstyles

The Earthen bring unique racial abilities and a rich cultural background that adds depth to the World of Warcraft universe. Their capital city, Dornogal, represents a new era of cooperation between factions.

Hero Talents: Rise to New Heights of Power

The Hero Talents system represents a major evolution in character customization, offering new ways to specialize and enhance your playstyle. This system draws inspiration from iconic Warcraft universe archetypes.

Hero Talent System Features

  • Three Trees per Class: Each class features three distinct Hero Talent trees
  • Two Active Trees: Players can select two out of three trees per specialization
  • Automatic Unlock: Hero Talent trees unlock at level 71
  • Progressive Points: Earn one talent point per level from 71-80
  • Multiple Choice Nodes: Select between different talents for customization
  • Low Respec Cost: Encourages experimentation with different builds

Each Hero Talent tree contains ten talents with multiple choice nodes, allowing for extensive personalization. Whether you dream of embodying the valor of a knight, the cunning of a rogue, or the arcane mastery of a mage, Hero Talents provide the tools to bring your character's fantasy to life.

Warbands: Account-Wide Progression Revolution

The Warbands system revolutionizes alt management by introducing comprehensive account-wide progression across your family of characters, regardless of faction.

Warband Features

  • Warband Bank: Multiple tabs for storing reagents and shared resources
  • Transferrable Gear: Warbound items can be shared between characters
  • Account-Wide Reputation: Reputation progress shared across all characters
  • Transmog Collections: Unlocked appearances available account-wide
  • Flight Paths: Discover once, access everywhere
  • Delves Progress: Dungeon exploration shared across characters
  • Achievements: Track accomplishments across your entire roster

This system eliminates the tedious micromanagement of multiple characters, allowing players to focus on adventures rather than repetitive tasks. The Warband Bank ensures efficient resource management across your entire character roster.

Delves: A New Endgame Pillar

Delves represent an exciting new endgame feature designed for 1-4 players, offering outdoor vaults scattered throughout Azeroth filled with hidden secrets and valuable rewards.

Delve System Features

  • Flexible Group Size: Solo play or groups of up to 4 players
  • Role-Agnostic: Any specialization can participate effectively
  • NPC Companion: Brann Bronzebeard assists in your adventures
  • Seasonal Integration: Exclusive rewards each season
  • Great Vault Rewards: New outdoor row for weekly progression
  • Scalable Difficulty: Content adapts to group size and skill level

Dungeons and Raids: Epic Challenges Await

The War Within features eight new dungeons and multiple raid experiences, offering content for players of all skill levels.

Leveling Dungeons (70-80)

  • The Rookery: Navigate aerial challenges in this sky-bound fortress
  • The Stonevault: Delve into ancient titan vaults filled with mechanical guardians
  • Priory of the Sacred Flame: Explore this holy sanctuary under siege
  • Ara-Kara, City of Echoes: Venture into the abandoned nerubian city

Max-Level Dungeons

  • Cinderbrew Meadery: Battle through this dwarven establishment
  • Darkflame Cleft: Face the dangers of this volcanic chasm
  • The Dawnbreaker: Board this massive airship for aerial combat
  • City of Threads: Navigate the political intrigue of nerubian society

Season 2 Dungeon Rotation

Season 2 features a refreshed Mythic+ dungeon pool:

  • Operation: Floodgate (New) - Race against time to stop dam sabotage
  • Cinderbrew Meadery - Dwarven brewery chaos
  • The Rookery - Aerial fortress challenges
  • Darkflame Cleft - Volcanic dangers

  • The MOTHERLODE!! (Battle for Azeroth) - Goblin mining operation

The Skittering Battlements

  • The Bloodbound Horror: A vile creature rising from Old God blood

Secrets of Nerub-ar Palace

  • Rasha'nan: A proud nerubian transformed into a winged horror
  • Broodtwister Ovi'nax: A corpulent experimenter with black blood
  • Nexus-Princess Ky'veza: A mysterious confidante with deadly secrets

A Queen's Fall

  • Queen Ansurek: The paranoid ruler who has delved into dark powers

  • Setting: The Ringing Deeps, within the Gallagio
  • Story: Gazlowe and the goblins must rid themselves of Jastor Gallywix

  • Puzzling Cartel Chips: New dinar currency for targeted gear acquisition

Professions: Crafting Your Legacy

The War Within introduces significant updates to the profession system, building upon the foundation established in Dragonflight with new specializations, materials, and crafting opportunities.

Profession System Overview

  • Concentration System: Spend concentration to guarantee higher quality crafts
  • Specialization Trees: Deep customization for each profession
  • Quality Materials: Rank 1-3 materials affect final product quality
  • Crafting Orders: Public and personal order system for custom items
  • Profession Equipment: Specialized gear to enhance crafting abilities

Gathering Professions

Herbalism

Three specialization trees focus on different aspects of herb gathering:

  • Bountiful Harvests: Refine herbs to higher quality ranks
  • Botany: Mounted gathering and herb-specific bonuses
  • Overloading the Underground: Specialized underground gathering techniques

Key Herbs: Mycobloom, Blessing Blossom, Arathor's Spear, Luredrop, Orbinid, and the rare Null Lotus

Mining

Mining specializations mirror herbalism structure:

  • Plethora of Ore: Refine ores and access to Null Stones
  • Mining Fundamentals: Mounted mining and efficiency improvements
  • Mastering the Mysterious: Advanced mining techniques

Key Ores: Bismuth (common), Aqirite, Ironclaw, plus valuable Null Stones

Skinning

Skinning focuses on leather and chitin processing:

  • Tanning: Process both Luxurious Leathers and Concrete Chitin
  • Harvesting: Gather additional cooking and crafting materials
  • Luring: Attract specific creatures for optimal skinning

Crafting Professions

Alchemy

Alchemy features powerful transmutation abilities:

  • Thaumaturgy: Discover new transmutation recipes
  • Potent Potions: Enhanced combat consumables with bulk production
  • Fantastic Flasks: Long-duration stat enhancement flasks

Key Feature: Blasphemite creation through Gleaming Transmutagen

Blacksmithing

Blacksmithing excels in both gear and tool creation:

  • Means of Production: Craft profession equipment and tools
  • Armorsmithing: Create plate armor for warriors and paladins
  • Weaponsmithing: Forge powerful weapons and shields

Leatherworking

Leatherworking serves both armor crafting and profession support:

  • Luxurious Leathers: Craft leather armor for rogues, monks, demon hunters, druids
  • Concrete Chitin: Create mail armor for hunters, shamans, evokers
  • Flawless Fortes: Profession equipment and embellished gear

Tailoring

Tailoring focuses on cloth armor and magical enhancements:

  • Textile Treasures: Maximize cloth gathering from humanoids
  • Quality Fabric: Spellthread and enhancement creation
  • From Dawn Until Dusk: Specialized bolt creation with daily cooldowns

Enchanting

Enchanting provides essential gear enhancements:

  • Everlasting Enchantments: Permanent gear improvements
  • Ephemerals, Enrichments, and Equipment: Temporary enhancements and oils
  • Supplementary Shattering: Efficient material processing

Jewelcrafting

Jewelcrafting creates gems and accessories:

  • Gemcutting: Cut gems for socket bonuses (Ruby, Emerald, Onyx, Sapphire)
  • Accessories: Rings, necklaces, and trinkets
  • Blasphemite Processing: Work with alchemist-created materials

Engineering

Engineering combines utility and profession support:

  • Engineered Equipment: Profession tools and accessories
  • Inventor's Necessities: Utility items and gadgets
  • Explosive Devices: Combat consumables and toys

Inscription

Inscription handles knowledge and enhancement creation:

  • Pursuit of Knowledge: Create Algari Treatises for profession advancement
  • Archival Additions: Darkmoon Card discovery and transcription
  • Runic Mastery: Weapon and armor enhancements

Secondary Professions

Cooking

Cooking receives major quality-of-life improvements:

  • Salvaging System: Gather cooking materials from any enemy
  • Hearty Meals: Persist through death (available at 25 skill)
  • Hearty Feasts: Group-wide persistent benefits (available at 100 skill)

Fishing

Fishing introduces community events and detailed tracking:

  • Fishing Journal: Detailed information about each fish and where to find them
  • Hallowfall Fishing Derby: Weekly 24-hour community competition
  • Mereldar Derby Marks: Special currency for cosmetics and recipes

Profession Equipment and Stats

Crafting Stats

  • Multicraft: Chance to create additional items (best for most crafts)
  • Ingenuity: Refunds concentration when it procs
  • Resourcefulness: Uses fewer materials when crafting
  • Crafting Speed: Faster completion times

Gathering Stats

  • Perception: Increased chance to find rare materials
  • Deftness: Faster gathering speed
  • Finesse: Chance to gather additional basic materials

Artisan's Acuity

The War Within's equivalent to Artisan's Mettle, used for:

  • Purchasing blue profession equipment recipes
  • Buying rare crafting materials
  • Accessing advanced profession features
  • Free 200 Acuity: Available through early profession quests

Dynamic Flying: Soar Through Khaz Algar

The successful Dragonriding system from Dragonflight returns as Dynamic Flying, now available across all zones of Azeroth and enhanced for The War Within.

Dynamic Flying Features

  • Immediate Access: Available upon reaching Khaz Algar
  • Expanded Zones: Now works in Eastern Kingdoms, Kalimdor, Outland, and beyond
  • Dual Modes: Switch between Dynamic Flight and traditional "TBC Flying"
  • Evergreen Glyphs: Continuous progression system for enhancement
  • Druid Enhancement: Flight Form now supports Dynamic Flying
  • Mount Compatibility: Hundreds of mounts now support dynamic flying

This system provides unprecedented freedom and immersion as players journey across Azeroth's vast landscapes, making exploration more engaging than ever before.

Season 2: Undermine(d) - Current Content

Season 2 of The War Within, titled "Undermine(d)," launched with significant new content and features that expand the endgame experience.

New Raid: Liberation of Undermine

An 8-boss raid set in the goblin capital where players must help Gazlowe and the goblins rid themselves of Jastor Gallywix once and for all.

  • Location: The Ringing Deeps (Gallagio)
  • Difficulties: Story Mode, LFR, Normal, Heroic, Mythic
  • Item Level: LFR 606+
  • Special Features: Goblin-themed encounters and mechanics

New Mythic+ Affixes

  • Xal'atath's Bargain: Pulsar: Orbital mechanics requiring precise interception
  • Xal'atath's Guile: Scaling difficulty above level 12
  • Xal'atath's Bargain: Voidbound: Modified Dark Prayer mechanics

Season 2 Rewards

  • Crimson Shreddertank: Mount for 2000+ Mythic+ rating
  • Enterprising Shreddertank: Mount for 3000+ Mythic+ rating
  • Prized Gladiator's Fel Bat: PvP Elite mount
  • Vicious Electro Eel: Rated PvP mount (Horde/Alliance variants)

New PvP Arena: Cage of Carnage

A close-quarters gladiatorial arena in the Demolition Dome featuring trap doors and metal platforms with ramps at every corner.

New World Boss: The Gobfather

A malfunctioning mech from the Bilgewater Cartel that has gone haywire in the Demolition Dome, dropping Undermine-inspired armor pieces.

Upcoming Content: Patch 11.1.5 Nightfall

Patch 11.1.5 "Nightfall" introduces several major features rolling out over multiple weeks, bringing fresh content and quality-of-life improvements.

Content Release Schedule

  • April 22, 2025: Nightfall Scenario, Cooldown Manager, Winds of Mysterious Fortune
  • May 20, 2025: Horrific Visions Revisited
  • June 3, 2025: Dastardly Duos Event

Nightfall Scenario & Flame's Radiance

A new public scenario where players defend the Arathi against Sureki forces alongside the Flame's Radiance faction.

  • New Faction: Flame's Radiance with 10 Renown levels
  • Leaders: Thaed Pyremaker and Mylton Wyldbraun
  • Rewards: Armor sets, progressive tabard, weapons, Delver's Dirigible variant
  • Buffs: Increased power while in Hallowfall and Azj-Kahet

Winds of Mysterious Fortune Event

A month-long experience boost event (April 22 - May 20, 2025):

  • +20% Experience: For characters levels 10-79
  • +200% Dragonflight Renown: Major factions in Dragon Isles
  • +100% War Within Renown: Select major factions
  • Mysterious Satchels: Random drops with useful items and gear
  • Experience Potions: Additional +10% XP (stacks to 30% total)

Cooldown Manager

A new built-in UI feature for tracking class ability cooldowns:

  • Customizable HUD: Separate elements for different cooldown types
  • Visual Countdown: Clear indication of ability availability
  • Specialization-Specific: Automatically shows relevant abilities
  • Edit Mode Integration: Full customization through HUD Edit Mode

Horrific Visions Revisited (May 20)

The return of N'Zoth's corrupted Stormwind and Orgrimmar with modern updates:

  • Solo or Group Play: 1-5 players with optional Soridormi companion
  • Sanity Mechanic: Maintain sanity or face failure
  • Heroic Difficulty Gear: Up to Heroic raid-level rewards
  • Classic Masks: Pain, Dark Imagination, Long Night, Burned Bridge, Daredevil
  • New Masks: Vengeance, Multitudes, Nemesis
  • New Currency: Displaced Corrupted Mementos for rewards
  • Rewards: Mounts, toys, transmogs, weapon enchantments

Dastardly Duos Event (June 3)

A six-week event featuring boss encounters and scoring systems:

  • Animatronic Bosses: Fight recreated versions of past bosses
  • Multiple Bosses: Face 2+ bosses simultaneously
  • Three Hubs: Stormwind, Orgrimmar, and Dornogal
  • Weekly Progression: 4 bosses initially, up to 10 total
  • Scoring System: Points for kills, damage, and performance
  • Powerups: Special abilities and enhancements
  • Rewards: Cosmetics, toys, dungeon items

Puzzling Cartel Chips (Dinars)

A new bad luck protection system for Season 2:

  • Purpose: Purchase specific Liberation of Undermine gear
  • Acquisition: Earned through various Season 2 activities
  • Vendor System: Direct gear purchases from NPCs
  • Bad Luck Protection: Ensures eventual access to desired items

Getting Started in The War Within

To begin your journey in The War Within, ensure your character meets the level requirements and has completed the prerequisite content from previous expansions.

Prerequisites

  • Character Level: Level 70 (or use the included character boost)
  • Previous Content: Completion of Dragonflight campaign recommended
  • Subscription: Active World of Warcraft subscription required

First Steps

  1. Travel to Khaz Algar through the new portal system
  2. Begin the campaign in the Isle of Dorn
  3. Establish your base in Dornogal
  4. Unlock Dynamic Flying immediately
  5. Start exploring the four new zones
  6. Begin Hero Talent progression at level 71

Progression Path

  • Levels 70-80: Complete zone campaigns and side quests
  • Level 80: Begin endgame content (dungeons, delves, raids)
  • Gear Up: Use the new upgrade system with crests
  • Weekly Activities: Great Vault, world bosses, and seasonal events

Season 2 Specific Activities

  • Liberation of Undermine: New 8-boss raid experience
  • Updated Mythic+: Fresh dungeon rotation with new affixes
  • Enhanced Delves: New delves and increased difficulty
  • PvP Season 2: New arena and updated rewards
  • Profession Advancement: Continue specialization progression
